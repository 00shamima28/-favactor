Sivakarthikeyan

Indian actor|playback singer


Sivakarthikeyan is a popular Tamil actor, singer, and comedian. He was born on February 17, 1985. He married Aarthi in 2010, and they have three children: Aaradhana, Gugan, and Pavan. Sivakarthikeyan's father was a jail superintendent, and his mother is a homemaker. Sivakarthikeyan was born in Singampunari, Sivagangai district. He attended Campion Anglo Indian Hr. Sec. School in Trichy and later earned a Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science and Engineering from JJ College of Engineering and Technology. He also pursued an MBA from the Indian Institute of Planning and Management in Chennai.
